Rules

The game is divided into 2 phases:

  • build Collect-Phase

    The players can enter words into the fields. The goal is to to find words that the player uses as often as possible. Also actions, such as *drink* are allowed. Actions should be marked by asterisks (*), so that there is no confusion later. so that there is no confusion later. If a player cannot think of 25 terms, he can fill in the remaining fields automatically. fill in the remaining fields.

  • games Bingo-Phase

    This is where the real game begins. Someone streams and every time and every time a word is spoken or a player does something that the player has that player has previously entered, the player clicks on the respective field and it is marked as said or done.

    The first to have 5 in a row wins.

At the same time, the game currently runs a lot on trust, so find friends to play with. As long as the game does not have an AI that can understand the stream, there is unfortunately no other way.

Controls

Click on field Collect phase: Edit field
Bingo phase: Mark field
Enter Collect phase: Save word
Tab Collect phase: Save & next field
Shift + Tab Collect phase: Save & one field back
Esc Collect-Phase: Close edit
